## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit StudySmart**
   - Navigate to the StudySmart website
   - Click the "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button

2. **Sign Up**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm your password
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to log in

4. **First Login**
   - Enter your credentials
   - You'll be taken to your dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is divided into several sections:

- **AI Chat**: Get instant help from your AI tutor
- **Resources**: Organize your study materials
- **Goals**: Set and track learning objectives
- **Schedule**: Plan your study sessions
- **AI Solver**: Get step-by-step problem solutions
- **Progress**: Monitor your learning journey
- **Profile**: Manage your account settings

## AI Chat Tutor

### Starting a Conversation

1. Click the **"AI Chat"** tab in your dashboard
2. Type your question in the message box
3. Press Enter or click the send button
4. The AI tutor will respond in seconds

### Effective Questions

**Good Questions:**
- "Can you explain photosynthesis in simple terms?"
- "How do I solve quadratic equations?"
- "What's the difference between mitosis and meiosis?"
- "Can you help me understand Newton's First Law?"

**Less Effective Questions:**
- "Help" (too vague)
- "I don't get it" (no context)
- "Everything" (too broad)

### Features

#### Subject Selection
- Choose from multiple subjects
- Get specialized expertise for each topic
- Subjects include: Math, Science, History, Literature, and more

#### Conversation History
- All your conversations are saved
- Review past discussions anytime
- Continue where you left off

#### Teaching Style
The AI tutor uses the **Socratic Method**:
- Asks guiding questions
- Helps you discover answers
- Builds critical thinking skills
- Doesn't just give answers away

### Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Include what you already know and what you're struggling with
2. **Ask Follow-ups**: Don't hesitate to ask for clarification
3. **Share Your Work**: Show your attempt at solving a problem
4. **Request Examples**: Ask for real-world applications
5. **Break It Down**: Split complex topics into smaller questions

## AI Problem Solver

### Solving Text Problems

1. Click the **"AI Solver"** tab
2. Select your subject from the dropdown
3. Type or paste your problem in the text box
4. Click **"Solve Problem"**
5. Wait a few seconds for the solution

**Example Problems:**
```
Math: "Solve for x: 3x + 7 = 22"
Physics: "A car accelerates from 0 to 60 km/h in 5 seconds. Calculate the acceleration."
Chemistry: "Balance the equation: H2 + O2 → H2O"
```

### Solving Image-Based Problems

1. Click the **"AI Solver"** tab
2. Select your subject
3. Click the **"Upload Image"** button
4. Choose a photo of your problem
5. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, HEIC
6. Click **"Solve Problem"**

**Image Tips:**
- Take clear, well-lit photos
- Ensure text is readable
- Capture the entire problem
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Crop unnecessary parts

### Understanding Solutions

Each solution includes:

1. **Step-by-Step Breakdown**
   - Each step numbered
   - Clear explanation for each action
   - Mathematical work shown

2. **Final Answer**
   - Clearly marked
   - Includes units if applicable

3. **Difficulty Level**
   - Easy, Medium, or Hard
   - Helps gauge problem complexity

4. **Helpful Hints**
   - Additional tips
   - Common mistakes to avoid
   - Related concepts

### Session History

- Access past problems anytime
- Review solutions
- Learn from previous mistakes
- Delete sessions you don't need

## Study Goals

### Setting Goals

1. Go to **"Goals"** tab
2. Click **"Add New Goal"**
3. Enter goal details:
   - **Title**: What do you want to achieve?
   - **Subject**: Which subject area?
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Target Date**: When do you want to complete it?
4. Click **"Save Goal"**

### SMART Goals

Make your goals **SMART**:

**S - Specific**
- ❌ "Get better at math"
- ✅ "Master quadratic equations"

**M - Measurable**
- ❌ "Study more"
- ✅ "Complete 20 practice problems"

**A - Achievable**
- ❌ "Learn all of calculus in one day"
- ✅ "Understand derivatives by end of week"

**R - Relevant**
- ❌ "Learn ancient Greek" (if you're studying chemistry)
- ✅ "Understand chemical bonding for exam"

**T - Time-bound**
- ❌ "Eventually understand physics"
- ✅ "Complete physics chapter 5 by Friday"

### Tracking Progress

**Goal Status**
- **Not Started**: Goal is set but no work yet
- **In Progress**: Actively working on it
- **Completed**: Goal achieved!

**Updating Progress**
- Click on a goal
- Mark milestones as complete
- Update status as you progress

**Celebrating Success**
- Completed goals are celebrated
- Track your achievements
- Stay motivated!

### Goal Examples

**Mathematics**
- "Complete 50 algebra problems by end of month"
- "Score 90% or higher on geometry quiz"
- "Master fraction operations in 2 weeks"

**Science**
- "Memorize periodic table by Friday"
- "Understand photosynthesis process"
- "Complete biology lab report"

**Language Arts**
- "Read 3 chapters of assigned novel"
- "Write 5-paragraph essay on Shakespeare"
- "Learn 50 new vocabulary words"

## Study Schedule

### Creating Your Schedule

1. Navigate to **"Schedule"** tab
2. View the weekly calendar
3. Click **"Add Study Session"**
4. Fill in details:
   - **Subject**: What will you study?
   - **Day**: Which day of the week?
   - **Start Time**: When do you begin?
   - **End Time**: When do you finish?
5. Click **"Save"**

### Weekly Planning

**Balanced Schedule**
- Don't overload any single day
- Mix difficult and easy subjects
- Include breaks between sessions
- Plan for 7 days ahead

**Time Blocks**
- 30-minute sessions for intense subjects
- 45-60 minute sessions for reading/writing
- 15-minute review sessions

**Subject Rotation**
- Alternate subjects throughout the week
- Study challenging subjects when alert
- Review easier subjects when tired

### Schedule Best Practices

**Consistency**
- Study at the same time each day when possible
- Build a routine
- Make it a habit

**Peak Performance**
- Schedule difficult subjects during your peak hours
- Morning person? Study math early
- Night owl? Review notes before bed

**Regular Breaks**
- 5-10 minute break every hour
- Longer break after 3-4 hours
- Avoid burnout

**Realistic Planning**
- Don't over-schedule
- Allow buffer time
- Account for other commitments

### Managing Your Schedule

**Editing Sessions**
- Click on any scheduled session
- Update time or subject
- Save changes

**Deleting Sessions**
- Remove completed sessions
- Clear outdated schedules
- Stay organized

**Recurring Sessions**
- Set up repeating study times
- Automatically copies to next week
- Easy schedule maintenance

4. Use flashcards
5. Test yourself regularly

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Log In
**Problem**: Wrong email or password
**Solution**:
1. Check for typos
2. Try "Forgot Password"
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Clear browser cache

#### AI Not Responding
**Problem**: No response from AI tutor
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try again in a few minutes
4. Check if service is down
5. Contact support if persists

#### Image Upload Fails
**Problem**: Can't upload problem image
**Solution**:
1. Check file size (max 10MB)
2. Use supported formats (JPG, PNG)
3. Ensure image is clear
4. Try different image
5. Use text input instead

#### Slow Performance
**Problem**: App is slow or laggy
**Solution**:
1. Close unnecessary tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet speed
4. Update browser
5. Try different browser

#### Missing Data
**Problem**: Resources or goals disappeared
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check filters/search
3. Verify you're logged in
4. Check different tabs
5. Contact support

### Error Messages

**"Session Expired"**
- Log out and log back in
- Your session timed out for security

**"Network Error"**
- Check internet connection
- Try again in a moment
- Service might be temporarily down

**"Rate Limit Exceeded"**
- You've made too many requests
- Wait a few minutes
- Try again

**"Upload Failed"**
- File might be too large
- Use supported format
- Try different file

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is StudySmart free?**
A: Yes! Basic features are free. Premium features available with subscription.

**Q: What subjects are supported?**
A: Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), History, Literature, Languages, and more.

**Q: Can I use StudySmart on mobile?**
A: Yes! The website is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your data.

**Q: Can teachers use StudySmart?**
A: Yes! Teachers can monitor student progress and assign resources.

### AI Tutor Questions

**Q: How smart is the AI tutor?**
A: Very! It uses advanced AI models trained on vast educational content.

**Q: Can the AI do my homework?**
A: The AI helps you learn, not cheat. It guides you to find answers yourself.

**Q: Does the AI remember our conversations?**
A: Yes, within each session. It uses context from previous messages.

**Q: Can I ask questions in other languages?**
A: The AI primarily works in English, but can handle basic translations.

**Q: Is there a limit to questions?**
A: Free users have daily limits. Premium users get unlimited questions.

### Problem Solver Questions

**Q: What types of problems can it solve?**
A: Math, Physics, Chemistry problems. Text or image based.

**Q: How accurate are the solutions?**
A: Very accurate, but always double-check critical work.

**Q: Can it solve word problems?**
A: Yes! It handles word problems across all subjects.

**Q: What if it gets a solution wrong?**
A: Report it! We continuously improve the AI.

**Q: Can it read handwriting?**
A: Yes, but clear handwriting works best. Print is even better.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - all modern browsers.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No! StudySmart works entirely in your browser.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Not yet, but offline mode is planned for the future.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Email support@studysmart.com with details.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Export Data.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I change my email?**
A: Go to Profile > Account Settings > Update Email.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Settings > Account > Delete Account (permanent).

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Each email can have one account. Different emails = different accounts.

**Q: How do I upgrade to premium?**
A: Click "Upgrade" button in your dashboard.
